## Energy from carbs
Carbohydrates are one of the three energy-yielding macronutrients.  They're an important energy source for the whole body.  Preferred energy source for nerve cells (brain).  They contain H2O and CHO.

Carbs can be [[Simple carbohydrates|simple]] (sugars) or [[Complex carbohydrates|complex]] (long chains).

## Photosynthesis
We get carbs primarily from plant foods like fruits, vegetables, or grains.  Plants make **glucose** (most abundant monosaccharide), through photosynthesis.  In this process the green pigment of plants absorbs sunlight, which provides the energy needed to fuel the manufacture of glucose.  Oxygen is produced as the byproduct of this reaction.
