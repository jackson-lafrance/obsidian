## What are lipids
Lipids are a large and diverse group of substances that only share the fact that they are all insoluble in water.  Lipids are stored in **adipose tissues** in the body that protect and insulate organs, they are combined with phosphorous in cell membranes, and also occur as steroids in bile salts, sex hormones, and other substances.  In foods lipids are both fats and oils, fats are solid at room temp while oils are liquid.

## Three types
Three types of lipids are commonly found in foods and in the cells and tissues of the human body:
- **[[Triglycerides]]**
- **[[Phospholipids and sterols|Phospholipids]]**
- **[[Phospholipids and sterols|Sterols]]**
